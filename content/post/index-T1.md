# Legend Chapter 795

Dropping rocks at the back of the punitive force caused injuries as well as significant confusion.

That was Rei’s goal. As soon as he finished dropping all the rocks he had, he planned to attack with his fire magic or maybe Overlord’s Armour.

If he he managed to do that, it would have caused a considerable disturbance among the punitive force, giving Mercurio’s army a bigger advantage.

And, in that sense, it would also decided the outcome of the battle. ……Rei wasn’t wrong in thinking that.

The fact was that if the punitive force had been organised the same way as before, they would have been powerless against Rei’s offensive and would have taken a one sided beating.

There was only one miscalculation in Rei’s thinking.

That there would be someone even stronger than Rei present in the punitive force, Noiz.

As Rei was taking rocks out of the Misty Ring, he felt a sudden chill down his spine.

At the same time, he saw something flying straight towards him……or more accurately, straight towards Set.

「Set-!」

Rei shouted out immediately, but it was already too late.

Set instinctively tried to flap his wings after hearing Rei’s voice as the object tore through the air towards his body.

The next moment, the tip of the spear pierced into Set’s body. ……No, just as it was about to pierce into Set, it seemed to collide with a wall of air, deflecting it.

「-! I see! Set, go down!」

Rei realised that the attack had been deflected due to the effect of Set’s Bracelet of Wind Manipulation, a magic item that could deflect a single projectile or magic attack before needing to recharge, and immediately told Set to fly down.

「Gururu~!」

Set seemed to realise how powerful the attack had been as well.

He flapped his wings as he descended to the ground.

He had manged to avoid the attack thanks to the Bracelet of Wind Manipulation.

However, while the bracelet could deflect projectiles or magic, it could only do so once.

After being used, it couldn’t be used again for 10 hours.

In other words, Rei and Set’s position in the sky currently made them targets of such attacks from the ground.

Ordinary bows and catapults might not even reach Set at his current height.

And, even if they did, a strike from the Death Scythe or Set’s claws would have been enough to block them.

However……that previous attack was different.

It was powerful enough that Rei and Set would only have been able to deal with it if they placed all their attention on the attack.

If it had been thrown at them in a head on fight, they might have been able to manage……but it had been thrown from the ground among countless soldiers, so they couldn’t identify where the attack had come from.

「Tch, I can’t believe there’s someone with that much strength in the punitive force.」

As they rapidly approached the ground, Rei suddenly realised something.

「I see. Are they the one? The one that the punitive force prepared to deal with me? Wasn’t it meant to be Rhodes?」

Reaching a strange understanding, he soon found himself close to the ground.

「Set-!」

Even without a clear command, Set understood what Rei meant from just that.

Just before hitting the ground, Set swung his front claws, using the soldiers below to cushion their fall.

The soldiers where naturally crushed beneath him, but Rei didn’t care as he jumped off Set’s back.

Pulling the Death Scythe out from the Misty Ring, he slashed out.

The heads of several nearby soldiers flew into the air as blood spurted from their necks.

The soldiers in the surrounding area had no idea what was happening.

Rocks had started to fall from the sky and as they tried to avoid them, a Griffon suddenly showed up. It was impossible to expect them to be able to respond quickly.

「……Hii-Hiiii!」

A nearby soldier was in shock, not understanding what had just happened, but as soon as they saw the 2m long scythe, they realised who was standing before them.

「It’s Crimson-! Crimson is here-!」

The effect of their words was immediate.

Everyone in the surroundings quickly distanced themselves from Set and Rei.

「Wow, they moved quite quickly……」

Rei let out a smile and muttered to himself as he readied the Death Scythe.

「But, if you think that just putting some distance between you and me will do anything……you’re naive! Flying Slash!」

With those words, a flying slash flew out from the Death Scythe, slicing towards the soldiers.

The power of the slash wasn’t fatal as long as they were wearing some kind of armour, but it would also hit multiple people..

Under normal circumstances, it would only have been enough to take them out of combat.

However……as Rei was the one attacking, it also threw all the soldiers into a state of panic.

「AA-AAAAAAAAH-!」

「It’s Crimson, Crimson is here!」

「The Griffon is here! Run, run now!」

They shouted as they ran away, opening a 10m ring of space around him.

Rei glanced at the desperately fleeing soldiers before smiling slightly as he spoke.

「Your choice is not wrong. But, I’ll just let you wear yourselves out. Set, take your time.」

「Gururu~!」

Hearing Rei’s words, Set gave a short cry before immediately lunging at a soldier.

The soldier, who had managed to keep their spear at a ready position while distancing themselves from Rei and Set, thrust his weapon towards Set on reflex as he charged towards him.

However, the spear was thrust out as he was moving backwards……and it was also an iron spear, without any magic metal, so it was impossible for it to stop Set. A single strike from from Set’s claws easily broke the spear, sending it flying from their hands with the tip piercing into a nearby unfortunate soldier.

「Eh?」

For a moment, the soldier let out a stupefied voice, unable to understand what had just happened. But, the next moment, a slash from Set’s claws shattered his head, killing him.

「Hii-, Hiiiii-!」

The soldiers, already terrified by Set’s presence, had flesh, bone, blood, brains, and eyes splattered to their faces after Set’s attack. It was impossible for them to keep fighting as they just screamed.

Set lunged at the screaming soldiers……or rather, the soldiers who had yet to give up their fighting spirit.

The frightened soldiers on served to spread more fear and confusion to others around them.

Set knew that, which was why he targeted the soldiers who were still somewhat able to fight.

A short distance away from where Set was rampaging around, Rei was wrecking havoc of his own.

However, the damage Rei was inflicting on his surroundings was incomparable to Set’s.

That was because Rei’s body was covered in compressed magic power to the point that it was visible.

He had activated Overlord’s Armour.

Furthermore, learning from his mistake in his previous fight at the then rebel army camp, he was also actively using the Bracelet of Magic Absorption.

He reaped the lives of soldiers as he swung the Death Scythe around in that state.

(As expected, absorbing magic power from mere soldiers can’t keep up with my consumption.)

Rei’s Death Scythe cut down a hesitant soldier who thrust out their spear and muttered to himself as he felt the magic power flow into him from the Death Scythe.

Pushing against the ground, Rei leaped into the air.

The next moment, several arrows pierced his previous location as he activated the Boots of Sleipnir. Dashing through the air, he landed in front of the archers who had been shooting arrows at him.

「Fuu~!」

With a sharp exhale, the Death Scythe slashed out horizontally.

As the bodies of several archers were sliced apart, Rei once again felt magic power flow into his body.

However, it wasn’t enough to maintain Overlord’s Armour. Deciding that he needed to defeat more enemies, Rei was about to make his next attack when……

A chill.

He felt something cold, as if a block of ice had been dropped down his back, and he stopped moving.

Normally, he would have deactivated Overlord’s Armour if he wasn’t moving so as to not create more strain on his body, but the chills down his spine were so intense that he didn’t even think of that.

A soldier was decapitated by the Death Scythe as they tried to run away and at almost the same time, a man leaped over the surrounding soldiers before landing in front of Rei.

「Heh. You’ve gotten so much better at using Overlord’s Armour compared to before. In such a short time too……I have to say, you’re impressive.」

The man muttered, genuinely impressed. Like Rei, he was also clad in Overlord’s Armour, magic power compressed to the point that it was visible.

……Yes, according to Rei’s knowledge, this was someone that shouldn’t have been here.

His appearance caused Rei to freeze for a moment.

If his opponent……Noiz, had wanted to, Rei could have died that instant……or at least suffered significant injuries.

However, Noiz’s goal wasn’t to take down Rei by surprise. He wanted to see how strong Rei had grown in this short period of time……and above all, to be able to fight Rei, Crimson, in a no holds barred fight, unlike at the tournament.

That was why Noiz held a ferocious smile while using Overlord’s Armour himself.

Indeed, from his perspective, Rei was still quite immature.

Overlord’s Armour was still being activated by brute force, consuming an obscene amount of magic power.

Still, compared to when they had fought in the tournament, Rei’s control of Overlord’s Armour had clearly improved.

(The amount of magic power consumed shouldn’t be insignificant……but he really has an absurd amount of magic power.)

Noiz muttered to himself, half in admiration and half in astonishment as he drew his beloved sword from the sheathe at his waist.

「Now……I didn’t get to experience your full strength in the last fight. Let me see it here. ……You guys, stand back. If you get caught up in a fight between Rei and I……you’ll die.」

Noiz’s words caused the soldiers to panic as they ran even further away than before.

In stark contrast to the soldiers, Set, who had been rampaging around earlier, stepped forward next to Rei.

「Gururururururururururu~!」

A cry filled with hostility.

Set’s cry displayed no sign of carelessness.

He stepped beside Rei prepared to fight with all his might.

「Oh, so that’s the Griffon? ……Compared to Griffon’s I’ve fought before, it seems much smarter. And I even sense a strange power from it. ……Is it really just an ordinary Griffon?」

Rei seemed surprised at Noiz’s words.

He was surprised by the fact that he had recognised in a single glance that Set wasn’t an ordinary Griffon and even more so that Noiz had fought Griffons before.

Needless to say, as A rank monsters, Griffons were rare.

Having combat experience against such monsters meant that his S rank status wasn’t just for show.

「If you think of him as my familiar, I think you’ll understand that he’s no ordinary Griffon.」

Saying that, Rei slowly moved the Death Scythe in his hands.

Readying to swing the blade down, intending to slice Noiz in half in a single blow.

「Well, I suppose that’s true. Even if you ask me before fighting, there’s no way I’d tell you my strengths. Besides, it really is rue to try and find out your opponent’s abilities before a fight, isn’t it?」

Noiz postured the magic sword in his hand lightly as he spoke.

Noiz’s response, as if showing that he had already predicted Rei’s attack, was a testament to the countless battles he had survived.

It was true that Rei had been in Elgin for nearly two years now.

The battles he had experience in that time were so intense that his combat experience couldn’t be called shallow.

But……even so, it had only been two years. To be exact, it would be two years by next spring.

In comparison, Noiz had been an adventurer for many years.

The battle experience he had collected before reaching S rank and the battle experience he had obtained after reaching S rank.

Both of those gave Noiz and incomparable edge compared to Rei in terms of combat experience.

(If I think about it purely from an experience perspective, there’s no way I can beat Noiz. But, if I could, it would be because……)

The battles Rei had fought up to this point had been supported by instantaneous flashes of inspiration, a sort of sense for combat.

This was in addition to his magic power, and above all……

「Set.」

「Gururu~!」

Set, his partner.

If they combined their powers, it would definitely not be impossible to defeat Noiz.

Reaching that conclusion, Rei fully unleashed his Overlord’s Armour as he charged forward.

Swinging the Death Scythe down from up high.

Rei charged forward with the Death Scythe in hand.

Noiz responded with his own magic sword.

Crimson and Immovable. The two, both carrying titles, clashed on the battlefield of the final battle in the Bestir Empire’s civil war.

Rei and Noiz clashed in the middle of the punitive force’s rear guard.

Both of them were using Overlord’s Armour, compressing their magic power to the point it visible, clashing in a 100m open space after Noiz warned the soldiers to leave.

Rei’s weapon, the Death Scythe, was named after the grim reaper’s weapon……and was also a symbol representing Crimson.

Clad in Overlord’s Armour, Rei poured his magic power into the Death Scythe as he unleashed a sharp and powerful blow, strong enough to cut through not just metal armour, but even armour made with magic metals like Mithril and Orichalcum.

However……

A high pitched metallic screech echoed out.

The soldiers watching on held their breathes, captivated by the sight unfolding before their eyes……as if their souls had been stolen away.

Their eyes could barely sense what was happening.

The Death Scythe and magic sword were swung at high speed.

From the outside, the fight between Noiz and Rei looked like a kind of dance.

However, it wad a dance where one wrong move could lead to the death of either dancer.

Even as they fought a battle that ordinary soldiers could understand, both Rei and Noiz held no signs of desperation on their faces.

To them, this was merely a warm up. It was also a way for them to gauge their opponent’s condition and their own condition.

For Noiz, it was to see how much Rei had grown since the finals of the fighting tournament, and for Rei, it was a way of checking how much the skill gap between he and Noiz had narrowed.

Everyone around them……not just the ordinary soldiers, but even skilled knights, would have struggled to believe what they were seeing.

Neither of them said a word. Yet, by the clashes of their weapons, they could sense each other’s intentions.

As a result……

(I guess I’m still no match in terms of skill.)

Even as they exchanged attacks all over the place, up, down, left, and right, Rei calmly confirmed the thoughts in his mind.

He still couldn’t keep up with Noiz in terms of pure skill.

The feel of his attacks as they made contact made that clear.

Noiz had been able to counter every attack Rei unleashed.

Neither of them had used their full strength yet.

It was clear that each of them still had several trumps cards up their sleeves, but Rei could still instinctively tell that there was a huge difference between their skill levels.

(Of course, that was something I knew from the start. Still, if Mercurio’s army is to win, I absolutely need to keep this guy under control……)

Rei crouched down to avoid the magic sword as it slashed at his neck and thrust the Death Scythe’s shaft towards Noiz’s belly.

Noiz deflected Rei’s attack with another swing of his magic sword before putting some distance between them.

Taking stock of his surroundings, Rei noticed that the punitive force’s rear guard, excluding the ones watching their duel, were preparing to attack Mercurio’s army.

(I can’t just focus on Noiz. Right now, I need to inflict damage on the punitive force as well. In other words……)

Quickly deciding on what he needed to do, Rei poured magic power into the Death Scythe.

『Flames, burn the enemy according to my will.』

The short incantation, finished in an instant, created a fireball at the butt of the Death Scythe.

『Fireball.』

With those words, Rei swung the Death Scythe, sending the fireball flying towards Noiz.

For a moment, Noiz didn’t seem to understand why Rei had launched a fireball at him, but after realising what was behind him, he swung his magic sword with a small smile on his face.

Seeing that, Rei grit his teeth at how his intention had been seen through so easily. He decided that what he needed to do now was to attack Noiz while also causing collateral to the surroundings. Putting his right had, which had been holding onto the Death Scythe, under his Dragon Robe, he activated Nebula’s Eye at his waist.

Grabbing hold of several arrowheads that appeared, Rei threw them out.

Naturally, the speed of the arrowheads was beyond what ordinary soldiers could respond to.

「Ahh-!」

「Eh!?」

「Ouch-! ……Eh? My hand……」

Of course, the arrowheads flying towards Noiz were all deflected by his magic sword, but his attack range wasn’t wide enough to deflect all of Rei’s attacks.

Noiz’s specialty was one on one combat.

Of course, he could always use Overlord’s Armour to charge into enemy lines, but his true strength was in single target attacks.

And, because of that, Noiz couldn’t properly deal with all of Rei’s arrowheads, which were fired in a wide arc.

It would have been a fatal blow to an ordinary human, but Noiz manage to block it with his magic sword.

Seeing what was happening, Noiz’s expression changed, albeit only for a moment, to a look of shock.

That was only natural. As his personal magic sword, Noiz’s weapon was incomparable to that of ordinary magic swords.

If someone were to do what Set had just done, it wouldn’t have been strange for the monster’s front legs to be sliced off.

But Set’s attack had matched that of the magic sword.

In other words, Set was just that strong.

Noiz swiftly jumped away a second time.

Next was Rei’s Death Scythe.

The ground where Noiz had been standing on was neatly sliced apart by the sharp attack.

「I see, your true strength lies in working together with the Griffon. And, it seems that this Griffon is quite different from ordinary Griffons.」

「Well, who knows. If you really want to know, why don’t you take a direct hit? You’ll probably find out then.」

Noiz smiled at Rei’s provocative words.

「That might be good, but first……I have something I need to do. Now, with Overlord’s Armour……let me see how well you’ve mastered it. I’m going to put in a bit of strength, so if you can’t keep up……you’ll die.」

With those words, Noiz disappeared from Rei’s sight in an instant.

Rei swung the Death Scythe out on reflex.

In an instant, a metallic clash echoed out into the surroundings.

「Yeah. Then the next thing I know, he was right next to Crimson……what’s going on?」

「I don’t know! Rather, make more space! If we keep staying here, we’ll get caught up and die!」

「No, wouldn’t it just be better for everyone to leave?」

「No! It’s only because we’re here that their fight has been limited to this space. If we let Crimson move freely from here, the one on one fight between the two will rapidly cross the entire battlefield in a blink of an eye! If that happens, the rear guard of our army will suffer overwhelming casualties!」

The commander shouted sharply.

His words were, in effect, telling the soldiers to form a human wall to protect the rest of the rear guard from Rei and Noiz’s fight. As soldiers in the army, they couldn’t disobey their superior’s orders.

It might have been a different situation if the army was in chaos and on the edge of defeat, but the battle had only just begun.

The soldiers in the rear guard couldn’t see the state of the rest of the battlefield, but it was hard to imagine that their own army was on the verge of defeat.

In other words, the soldiers unlucky enough to still be around had no choice but to help form the human wall.

As the soldiers cursed their bad luck, the battle between Rei and Noiz intensified.

「Gurururururu~!」

Set gave a loud cry as he struck out with is claws, but Noiz easily dodged it.

Just as he was about to drive his magic sword into Set’s body, he suddenly thrust it out sideways.

The next moment, Rei’s Death Scythe collided with the magic sword, sending out a high pitch screech.

The next moment, Rei and Noiz, who had been standing next to each other, were several dozens of meters away from each other before clashing weapons again at a completely different location.

To anyone watching, it was a completely incomprehensible scene as the two seemed to blink in and out of existence at different locations.

That was only natural.

With each attack, they would move to a different location where Death Scythe and magic sword would clash, repeating again and again.

As they fought in 100 square meter space, the soldiers could hardly keep track of them with their eyes.

As the battle continued, the Death Scythe and magic sword once again clashed at the center of the clearing as Rei and Noiz came to a halt.

「Your skills have certainly improved compared to before. I’ll admit that……but honestly, I thought you’d be a little stronger.」

「Oh, really? Considering that, it doesn’t seem like you’re overwhelming me……! Penetrate!」

The skill powered up the Death Scythe, increasing the power behind Rei’s thrust, but by the time he made his attack, Noiz had already disappeared from his sight.

「Magic Shield!」

Rei immediately used another skill, Magic Shield, creating a shield of light……and leaping away as he heard a sound.

The next moment, a stone pierced where Rei had been standing just before.

「For an S rank adventurer, you’re pretty stingy, aren’t you!?」

Rei said that as he used the Death Scythe to cut apart the stone flying towards him.

It would be a waste to use Magic Shield against a stone like that.

With that thought in mind, he dashed in the direction the stone had come from.

Rei’s speed was so fast that he went unnoticed by the surrounding soldiers.

But even so……Noiz’s handling of Overlord’s Armour was still superior.

Even if their linear speed was the same, Noiz’s fine control was overwhelmingly higher.

The only reason Rei was able to match Noiz was thanks to his immense magic power.

He made up for his lack of fine control with sheer throughput of magic power.

It was a fighting style best described as overwhelming Noiz with brute force.

This was all only possible because Rei’s magic power vastly surpassed Noiz’s.

For every 10 units of magic power Noiz consumed, Rei would match with 100……or even 1000 units of magic power.

Naturally, using that much magic power to match meant the drain on Rei’s magic power was massive.

Overlord’s Armour was already formed by compressing magic power to the point it was visible, a skill that didn’t take magic power consumption into account.

Rei was forcibly activating Overlord’s Armour to keep it in that state, so he naturally couldn’t use it for very long.

That much was clear from his defense of the camp.

(Damn it, even with the Bracelet of Magic Absorption, it’s so difficult to even land a scratch. It’s just too difficult!)

Cursing internally at Noiz’s strength, Rei deflected the magic sword with the Death Scythe.

Deflecting one attack, moving, and deflecting another attack or attacking himself.

As their fight continued……Rei realised that if things stayed like this, he would eventually lose. And not because of being injured, but because he would run out of magic power first……and at that point, no matter what he did, it would be hard to turn the situation around.

(So, what should I do then? If I’m no match for Noiz on my own and I can’t defeat him……then I’ll have to find strength where I have it!)

Rei knew that this method would cause a lot of trouble later on.

But even so……Rei knew that if he didn’t try something different, he would lose, and if he lost, Mercurio’s army would lose as well. Shaking off his momentary hesitation, he shouted out.

「Set, use your skills!」

Use your skills.

Few would have understood what that meant when Rei said those words.

That was because there was a strong understanding among people that skills were things only people could use.

It was true that there were monsters that could manipulate the elements like wind and water, spitting fire, or using the ground to create spikes.

However, those were just special attacks unique to the monster’s race.

In other words, those attacks were similar to a human using their own fists to punch an opponent.

That was why no one could accurately understand what Rei meant by ‘use your skills’.

Also, did Set refer to someone else……maybe a soldier who was hiding nearby? There were a few soldiers who believed that, but many of them had heard Rei call out to his Griffon using the name Set. Above all, the punitive force had fought against Rei multiple times already.

Naturally, they had collected information on Rei as one of their top priorities and had provided such information to people above a certain rank.

The name of Rei’s tamed Griffon was clearly present in that information……making those people very confused as to the intentions of Rei’s words.

Noiz was equally confused, but from his experience as an S rank adventurer, he judged his position to be dangerous if he remained where he was and immediately jumped away.

At the same time……

「Gurururururururu~!」

With Rei’s permission, Set gave a loud cry before using Fire Breath towards the ground.

The flames flew towards where Noiz had just been, enveloping it with intense heat as the ground burned.

It was now autumn and the weather had cooled a lot.

However, the people present felt as if it had to returned to mid summer in an instant from the intense heat of Set’s fire breath.

This was an unexpected turn of events, even for Noiz.

He spoke with disbelief as he landed some distance from where the fire breath had landed.

「The Griffon can use Fire Breath?」

As Noiz muttered, somewhat in shock, he suddenly spun around as he swung his magic sword.

A high pitched metallic screech echoed out and Rei frowned as he spoke.

「If you were surprised for just a little longer, this would have been the only attack I needed.」

「Hmph, you’re giving off too much killing intent. No, you’ve suppressed your killing intent quite well, but you’re still too green as far as I am concerned.」

「Is that so……-!」

Using the momentum from the magic sword’s deflection of his attack, Rei tried to stab Noiz with a thrust of the Death Scythe’s shaft, but that attack was also met with the magic sword in a violent clash.

「I had a feeling it wasn’t an ordinary monster, but I can’t believe it can use Fire Breath. Is it a rare species of Griffon?」

「Hah-, well said. I don’t think you should be saying that after you dodged his attack so easily though. But……now that you’ve seen it, I’ll be giving my all as well. My title of Crimson was given to both me and Set. Set!」

「Gurururururu~!」

In response to Rei’s shout, Set used Fire Breath again from above.

The flames from his beak spread into the surroundings as it hit the ground.

It didn’t just reach Noiz either.

No matter how much space had been made for the two of them, it was still only a 100m square.

Since that was the case……Set’s fire breath started to head towards the wall of soldiers as the flames poured down.

「UWAAAAAAAAAAH-!」

「Get down, get down, get down! We’re going to get caught up in their fight!」

「Hot, hothothot! Someone please put out the fire, the fire!」

「Damn it! That Crimson, doing whatever he wants just because this is our camp!」

「Water, is there anyone who can use water magic!?」

「You think there are mages so conveniently around!? Use sand, put out the fires with sand and dirt on the ground!」

Despite the screams and confusion, the soldiers piled dirt on the comrades who were burning.

However, the flames from Set’s fire breath wouldn’t disappear so easily and those who were unlucky even had the flames on their body spread to the dead grass on the ground, spreading the carnage even further.

While the soldiers were thrust into what could only be described as screaming confusion……

「HAAAAAAAH-!」

「You think an attack like that will work? You’re not living up to your title of Crimson!」

Rei and Noiz were sweeping freely through the battlefield, which had since become a scorching hell, their weapons clashing everywhere.

With both of them using Overlord’s Armour, their movements couldn’t be followed by the soldiers’ eyes.

They rapidly blinked all over the place, fighting here for a moment, and there in the next.

All that said, this situation still placed Noiz at an advantage.

In fact, a magic shield that Rei had just put up had already been destroyed due to an attack from Noiz’s magic sword.

Adding on to that……

「Gururururu~!」

Set continued to use Fire Breath as he tracked Noiz in his Overlord’s Armour.

However, Fire Breath was an attack that required Set to breath in before using it, releasing flames from his beak.

In other words, there was a slight delay between Set using Fire Breath and the flames actually coming out.

With his ability to make high speed movements in Overlord’s Armour, Noiz could easily avoid it with the delay.

As a result, while Set’s Fire Breath couldn’t hit Noiz, it did cause significant damage to the soldiers in the surrounding area.

「Is that so? Then I’ll kick things up a notch. Set!」

「Gurururu~!」

「What-!」

The next moment, Set used Evil Eye Impact.

The power was quite weak, but unlike Fire Breath, the skill could be used instantly.

And, in this case, the instantaneous effect was the important aspect.

Even if the impact was light, it was sufficient to stop Noiz for a moment……for just a moment……

「I’ve got you!」

Not missing that moment, Rei swung the Death Scythe at Noiz.

There, the water dragon was looking back at her, as if ready to attack at any moment.

If the water dragon had been created using ordinary magic, it would remain under the control of the mage who created it.

In other words, the moment the mage lost consciousness, the water dragon would also disappear.

But, this water dragon hadn’t been created with ordinary magic, it was something formed from spirit magic.

In other words, it possessed it’s own consciousness.

That was why it didn’t disappear even after Digma lost consciousness. Instead, it remained ready to protect Digma, wary of Vihera, who was standing beside him.

「Don’t worry. I’m not going to kill him.」

「GYAAAA」

The water dragon gave a short cry.

It seemed to doubt whether Vihera was telling the truth.

However, Vihera didn’t seem particularly concerned about the water dragon as she picked up Digma.

「That said, i can’t leave him here like this. It would be a problem if he joined back up with the punitive force after waking back up, so I’ll have to take care of him for now.」

With those words, Vihera started heading back to her camp.

Perhaps believing Vihera’s words, the water dragon turned back into ordinary water as it collapsed to the ground in a large splash.
